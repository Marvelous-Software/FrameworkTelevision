New York City

One of my favorite places to go is New York City. If you want adventure, this is the place! You can easily get your fill of excitement with many options. There's a multitude of music venues, museums, parks, stores and much, much more! Here I will mention a few attractions at Hudson Yards.

The Vessel

The Vessel is a building and an artwork. It resembles a honeycomb lattice which is just lots of steps. You walk around, up and down and see great views. The only catch is reservations are required. The good news is the tickets are free!

The High Line

The High Line while not technically part of Hudson Yards, it has two entrances over in that area and I wish to include it. It is an "L" (elevated train) that was converted to a park. It provides good views of the Hudson River, many interesting peices of art displayed in the park and on various buildings along the way. There are also some cool buildings viewable on the trail. Plus there's the horizontal waterfall!

The Shed

The Shed is a building designed to host events. It can transform in a number of ways. I have yet to attend an event here, so I have something to look forward to!
